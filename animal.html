about us

Our Mission At Sarvek for Animal Welfare,

our mission is to end animal cruelty, engage NGOs and individuals together, promote neutering and spaying of animals, and save street animals.

We are dedicated to:

Ending Animal Cruelty: Advocating for laws and policies that protect animals from cruelty and abuse, and providing resources for the rescue and rehabilitation of mistreated animals.

Promoting Neutering and Spaying: Encouraging responsible pet ownership through initiatives that promote the neutering and spaying of animals to control overpopulation and improve their welfare.

Street Animals: Providing compassionate care, medical treatment, and shelter to street animals, and facilitating adoption programs to find them loving homes.Together, we strive to create a compassionate society where all animals are treated with dignity, respect, and kindness.
